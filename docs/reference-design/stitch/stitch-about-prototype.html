About

MONDAY MUSIC MIX is a personal archive for mixes built slowly and kept with care.

Why build it this way

Some projects work better when they start with the right room instead of rushed output. Monday Music Mix is being rebuilt as a place for hand-sequenced mixes, quiet notes, and listening that feels intentional rather than automatic.

What will live here

Finished mixes, tracklists, short notes, and whatever context still feels useful after the initial impulse wears off. Less feed, more archive.

On sequencing

When the mixes arrive, they’ll be assembled by hand and ordered with care. No fake lore, no manufactured backlog, no pretending this archive arrived fully formed.

Start small. Keep it real. Let the archive earn its shape.
